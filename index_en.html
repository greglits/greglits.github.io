Communication, Media, Environment, and the Digital Society


The perspective of this blog is that the digital has become an environment in which a large part of our lives and interactions now take place. The research I conduct at the Media and Journalism Observatory of UCLouvain aims to understand this new environment by exploring its connections with both social and natural environments.

This simple web page allows me to inhabit this environment and manage my identity in an independent and sustainable way, without having to rely on social media platforms..


Here, you will find a range of content and resources related to my work as a professor of digital media sociology and communication at UCLouvain.
