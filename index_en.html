Communication, Media, Environment, and the Digital Society

The digital realm has become an environment in which much of our lives and interactions now unfold. My research at UCLouvain’s Observatory for Media and Journalism seeks to better understand this new environment by examining its connections with both social and natural contexts, as well as the ways in which it reshapes our relationship to information, knowledge, and the media.

This website is my digital shelter in this environment, allowing me to manage my professional identity in an independent and sustainable way, without relying on social media platforms.

Here you will find a range of materials and resources related to my work as Professor of Digital Media Sociology and Communication at UCLouvain.
