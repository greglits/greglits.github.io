Grégoire Lits

I am a professor of media sociology at the University of Louvain, where I head the Observatory for Research on Media and Journalism. My work examines how information circulates in the public sphere: disinformation, media coverage of environmental and health crises, and trust in science.

Current research

My current research follows three complementary threads — the mediatisation of ecological transitions, vulnerability to disinformation, and the place of expertise in public debate.
