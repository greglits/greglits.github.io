Welcome!

I am a professor of media and digital sociology at the Université catholique de Louvain, where I direct the Observatory for Research on Media and Journalism. My work examines how information circulates in the public sphere: disinformation, media coverage of environmental and health crises, and trust in science and in journalismlism.

Current research

My current research explores three complementary directions — the media coverage of ecological transitions, vulnerability to disinformation, and the place of expertise in public debate.
