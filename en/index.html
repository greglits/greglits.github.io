Welcome!

I am Professor of Media and Digital Sociology at the Université catholique de Louvain and Director of the Observatory for Research on Media and Journalism. My research explores the dynamics of information circulation in the public sphere, focusing on disinformation, media representations of environmental and health crises, and the factors shaping public trust in science and the news media.

Current research

My current research explores three complementary directions — the media coverage of ecological transitions, vulnerability to disinformation, and the place of expertise in public debate.
