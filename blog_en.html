Do We Have a (Digital) Problem ?

Blog of Grégoire Lits
Professor of Media and Digital Sociology at UCLouvain

On this blog, you will find some reflections related to the research I conduct at the Media and Journalism Research Observatory at UCLouvain.

The common thread is that the "digital" has today become an environment in which our lives unfold, and it is important to understand how it transforms the ways we organize society and how we deal with the problems that affect us.

Currently, I am particularly interested in the mediatization of climate and environmental issues, as well as the evolution of our relationship with information, scientific knowledge, and the media.
